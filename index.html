This is a Micro-Jam game - I worked on it for ~5 hours (exclusive the initial idea).
It's a 4 player team game (2 vs. 2) that can be described as a very minimalistic football game.

Use eighter 4 PS3 controllers (left stick is used), or remap the controls in the standard unity interface. (e.g. shared controller).

Remark: I only have one controller at hand, and I could not test the gameplay with other people, so I have no idea how fun it is gameplay wise :D
